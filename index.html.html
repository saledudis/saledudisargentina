Academic validity

Our courses have formal university backing.

Through our framework agreement with the Universidad de la Marina Mercante, the training programs of Fundación SalEduDis grant certifications with recognized academic validity.

This positions our training well above the standard of most NGOs in the sector: those who train with us receive credentials that genuinely add value to their professional career.

Agreement signed in the City of Buenos Aires · June 2025

Italy · 2025

Guest Professor at PIS 2025 — Italy

Within the framework of the Italian conference "La sfida di fare sistema" (III Edition), Dr. Cangelosi Sánchez was invited as a Professore to lecture on educational inclusion before university audiences at the Università and in the city of Bagheria, Sicily.

His presentations were covered by Italian media — including a television interview — and recognized with institutional plaques by local authorities.

Research de tecnologías emergentes y Web3

Technical presentations at innovation forums

The Arq. Soft. Henry Tong Valera regularly participates as a speaker at Argentine and international technology ecosystem events: Hedera University Program, Argentine AI Society (SAIA), Cultura C3, NERD Conf and the UniBlock program (University Blockchain Network).

His international activity includes participation in North and Central America, Latam, Europe, China and Africa, consolidating the Technology pillar of the Foundation and ensuring the incorporation of digital innovation applied to inclusive solutions.

Featured project · Health

Ocular prosthetics: technology, craft and dignity.

Led by Lic. Sergio Aníbal Adra, specialist in visual rehabilitation, this project showcases the technical and human work behind the creation of custom ocular prosthetics.

It is a concrete example of how the Foundation articulates its areas: Health (specialized intervention), Education (training of new professionals) and Disability (comprehensive support for people with visual impairment).
